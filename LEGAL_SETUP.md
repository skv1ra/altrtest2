# Legal launch checklist

Before publishing, replace every `[ВКАЖІТЬ ...]` value in `lib/legal.ts` with the real controller details:

- full legal name or sole-proprietor name;
- registered address and country;
- working privacy and support email addresses;
- governing law and dispute venue.

Also complete these operational steps:

- connect the deletion-request form to a real server endpoint and monitored inbox;
- keep a server-side consent log with user id, policy version, timestamp, locale, and withdrawal history;
- list all actual processors, hosting regions, retention periods, and international-transfer safeguards;
- sign data-processing agreements with hosting, AI, payment, email, analytics, and integration providers;
- add the real payment, cancellation, refund, tax, and invoicing terms before charging users;
- do not enable analytics or marketing scripts until the cookie table and consent controls match them;
- perform a data-protection impact assessment before large-scale conversation analysis or sensitive-data processing;
- create incident response, access-control, backup deletion, and verified data-request procedures;
- obtain review by a qualified lawyer for the countries where Altr will be offered.
